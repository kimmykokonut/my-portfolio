About


I am a seeker and a life-long learner, with a desire to connect and understand what is around me.


Originally from the East Coast with strong opinions on bagels and pizza, I have put roots in the Pacific Northwest. My time here has filled my heart with the beauty of nature and my brain with the complexity of language learning.


I am a passionate and organized team player with track record of thriving both independently and collaboratively. I am currently advancing my skills in web and mobile development through a comprehensive full-time full-stack software engineering bootcamp at Epicodus. With a background in natural resources management and culinary arts, I bring a unique perspective to problem-solving and attention to detail. Excited to leverage my adaptable range of abilities and newly acquired coding skills in a software development role, where I can contribute to innovative solutions.


When I am not at the computer, I am probably in the forest: foraging for mushrooms, hiking to alpine lakes, or nordic skiing.
