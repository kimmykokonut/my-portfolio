About Kim


I am a continuous learner, with a desire to connect to and understand that which is around me.


Newly graduated from the final Epicodus cohort in Portland, I have a certificate in web and mobile development. It was a full-stack program with a focus on Javascript, React, C# and ASP.NET. I continue to learn and explore technology, having just finished my capstone project that included Python and a PostgreSQL database.

Drawing on my background in Natural Resources Management and Culinary Arts, I offer a unique approach to problem-solving and a keen eye to detail.


When I am not at the computer, I am likely in the forest: foraging for mushrooms, hiking to alpine lakes, or nordic skiing.
